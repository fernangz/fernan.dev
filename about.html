About Fernán García de Zúñiga

Hello! I'm Fernán García de Zúñiga, a passionate FrontEnd developer and designer with over two decades of experience in creating web experiences.

Throughout my career, I've worked on a wide variety of projects, from small personal websites to large-scale enterprise applications. My expertise lies in creating intuitive, accessible, and performant user interfaces.

What This Site Is About

This website serves as a platform to share my knowledge and thoughts about FrontEnd development. Here you'll find:

  • Tools: Practical utilities to help you in your daily FrontEnd work
  • Articles: Written content covering topics from basics to advanced concepts
  • Resources: A curated list of free tools and applications for developers

My Philosophy

I believe in creating websites that are:

  • Accessible: Everyone should be able to use the web, regardless of their abilities
  • Performant: Fast loading times and smooth interactions matter
  • Maintainable: Clean, well-organized code is essential for long-term success
  • Beautiful: Good design enhances user experience
