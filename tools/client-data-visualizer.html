About Client Data

Cookies: Small pieces of data stored by websites in your browser. They persist based on their expiration settings.

LocalStorage: A web storage mechanism that persists even after the browser is closed. Data has no expiration date.

SessionStorage: Similar to LocalStorage but data is cleared when the browser tab/window is closed.
