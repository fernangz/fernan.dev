SEO & LLMO Basics

Optimize for search engines and AI assistants.

Traditional SEO

Search Engine Optimization focuses on ranking well in search results. Key factors include quality content, proper heading structure, meta tags, internal linking, and page speed.

LLMO (Large Language Model Optimization)

LLMO is the emerging practice of optimizing content for AI assistants and chatbots. This includes clear, structured content, authoritative sources, and comprehensive topic coverage.

Best Practices

  • Use semantic HTML and proper heading hierarchy
  • Write clear, comprehensive content
  • Include structured data (Schema.org)
  • Optimize page speed and mobile experience
  • Build authoritative backlinks
  • Keep content updated and accurate
